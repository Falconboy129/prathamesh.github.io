About Me

I'm a junior at Sunset High School in Portland, OR, concurrently taking math and computer science courses at Portland State University. I enjoy building things — from AI-driven hardware to custom PCs — and am most interested in projects that solve real problems.

I'm the co-founder and captain of Mechanical Mages (FTC Team 20288), a non-profit organization that competes in FTC robotics tournaments and spreads robotics awareness in the community by running FLL summer camps for elementary schoolers, hosting mock FLL tournaments, and organizing official FLL tournament events. Outside of that, I'm a Top 20 Oregon varsity racquetball player and Oregon Open Badminton Champion (Men's Doubles, 2024). I also enjoy playing the piano and gaming in my freetime
