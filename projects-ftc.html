Overview

Mechanical Mages is a non-profit organization and FTC robotics team (Team 20288) competing across Sunset, Westview, and Jesuit High Schools in Portland, Oregon. Beyond competing, the organization is focused on spreading robotics awareness in the community — running FLL summer camps for elementary schoolers, hosting mock FLL tournaments, and organizing official FLL tournament events. I co-founded the team and lead all mechanical design, CNC fabrication, and strategic direction.
